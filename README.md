# Shore Guesthouse – Explore and Plan Your Stay

Welcome to **Shore Guesthouse**, your cozy seaside retreat. This project showcases the guesthouse’s offerings, gallery, and booking experience through a user-friendly website.

---

## **Project Overview**

The **Shore Guesthouse project** is a web-based platform designed to help users:

- Explore the guesthouse and its amenities
- Browse a gallery of rooms, surroundings, and experiences
- Plan and book their stay easily
- Learn about nearby attractions and services

The project emphasizes **visual appeal, smooth navigation, and responsive design** to create an engaging experience for visitors.

---

## **Features**

- **Home Page:** Introduction to Shore Guesthouse with highlights and call-to-actions
- **Gallery:** High-quality images of rooms, property, and surroundings
- **Booking/Planning:** Interactive section for planning your stay
- **About Us:** Information about the guesthouse and its story
- **Contact:** Easy contact form for inquiries and reservations
